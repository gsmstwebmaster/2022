Am I eligible to compete?
A team of 1-4 high school students is allowed to enter the challenge. All members must be 18 or younger to participate. We welcome coders from all skill levels to join and also attend a variety of workshops and guest speaker events which will be held throughout the hackathon.
About

Our mission is to recognize those who have extraordinary achievements in game design development and encourage others to pursue their passion for game design.
